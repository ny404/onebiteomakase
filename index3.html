THE "ONE BITE" PHILOSOPHY

The One Bite Omakase concept revolves around a dining experience where each meticulously crafted piece is designed to be enjoyed in a single, unforgettable bite. We focus on presenting a sequence of expertly prepared courses, each showcasing unique flavors and textures, curated by our skilled chefs.

Chef Johnny

Chef Johnny, a seasoned culinary professional, hails from Indonesia, where he was immersed in the culinary world from an early age through his mother's influence.

With over two decades of experience in the industry, he has honed his expertise, specializing in sushi for 15 years. He further expanded his repertoire by mastering desserts under the guidance of Chef Yoshie at Patisserie FOUET.

In 2009, Chef MASA personally entrusted him to assist in the opening of Bar MASA in Las Vegas, where he also mentored local recruits.

ESTEEMED CONTRIBUTIONS: Masa • Bar Masa • Hirohisa • Daniel

Chef Jason

Chef Jason has immersed himself in the sushi industry since 2010, fueled by a deep passion for Japanese cuisine and Omakase.

His enthusiasm for the culinary arts has remained unwavering over the years, bringing a dedicated energy to the counter every night.

RENOWNED ESTABLISHMENTS: Blue Ribbon • Bonito • Shinzo
